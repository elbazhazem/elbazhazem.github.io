# About Hazem Elbaz

👋 Hi, I’m **Hazem Elbaz** — a cybersecurity expert making a focused, deliberate transition into the world of **Artificial Intelligence**. My mission is to bridge the gap between cybersecurity and AI, creating smart, secure, and scalable solutions that harness the power of machine learning and large language models (LLMs).

---

## 🔁 From Cybersecurity to AI — Why the Shift?

Over the past years, I’ve developed deep expertise in cybersecurity — from network security and digital forensics to threat intelligence and anomaly detection. But the evolving threat landscape now demands more: **adaptive systems that learn, predict, and respond in real time**.

AI offers the tools to make that leap — and I’m committed to building those tools.

This isn’t a sudden pivot. It’s a **strategic evolution**: combining my cybersecurity background with AI to solve real-world security problems, before expanding into broader AI domains.

---

## 🧭 My Roadmap

Here’s how I’m transitioning from cybersecurity to AI:

### **Phase 1: AI *for* Cybersecurity** — *(now)*  
Building tools that use LLMs and ML models to analyze, summarize, and detect anomalies in security logs.

- ✅ **Project:** [Log Insight AI](https://github.com/elbazhazem/log-insight-ai)  
  *Using LLMs to summarize and interpret security log files — helping analysts focus on threats, faster.*

- 🔧 **Project (in progress):** [Log Anomaly Detection](https://github.com/elbazhazem/log-anomaly-detection)  
  *Applying clustering and classification algorithms to detect anomalous patterns — case study: DoS attacks.*

### **Phase 2: Research & Publishing** — *(upcoming)*  
- Finalizing a research paper:  
  *“Identifying Anomalous Network Traffic Through Clustering and Classification Methods: DoS Attacks as a Case Study”*

- Sharing open-source datasets, notebooks, and pipelines on GitHub to replicate experiments.

### **Phase 3: AI Core Skills & Applications** — *(mid-term)*  
- Mastering foundational AI topics:  
  - Machine learning pipelines  
  - NLP & vector embeddings  
  - LLM fine-tuning  
  - RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

- 📌 Projects in pipeline:  
  - **Auto-Incident Reporter:** an agent that reads logs and generates human-readable incident reports.  
  - **LLM for Threat Intel Enrichment:** extract and enrich IOCs from reports using LLM+NER.  
  - **Custom RAG System:** for querying internal security knowledge bases.

### **Phase 4: Beyond Security** — *(long-term)*  
Once my foundation in AI is solid, I’ll explore broader fields such as:  
- **AI agents** for automation  
- **AI in education and policy-making**  
- **Multimodal AI** for context-rich decision support
